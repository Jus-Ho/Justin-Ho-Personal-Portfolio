Greetings, welcome to my personal portfolio! My name is Justin Ho and I am a Computer Engineering and Computer Science student in the John Martinson Honors Program at Northeastern University in Boston, Massachussetts. During my studies, I aim to further my knowledge of the technical world through my coursework and clubs. In the near future, I would like to gain real-world experience and learn hands-on through a co-op experience in the Boston area.

In my short time at Northeastern, I have had the pleasure of expanding my realm of knowledge in a series of challenging and engaging technical courses. In addition to coursework, participation in events and clubs like Oasis at Northeastern have allowed me to learn by doing. Applying technical skills from coursework into individual and team based projects have widely broadened my technical abilities as well as interpersonal skills. As my education continues, I am eager to keep learning and expand upon my current skillset.

Outside of my studies and technical adventures, I have a deep passion for all things sports. I have been a life-long enjoyer of tennis, and most recently, pickleball. My passion for tennis has given me an expansive network of lifelong friends of which I have played both with and against, as well as unforgettable bonds with co-workers from my years as a tennis coach. As a student, tennis still remains part of my life as I currently play club tennis for Northeastern.
